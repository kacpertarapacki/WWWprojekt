Questions and Answers

1. What services do you offer?

We offer a variety of services including photography, videography, and event planning.

2. How can I book a service?

You can book a service by filling out the contact form on our website or by calling us directly.

3. What are your working hours?

Our working hours are from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.

4. What is your cancellation policy?

If you need to cancel, please notify us at least 48 hours in advance for a full refund.

5. Do you offer discounts for large bookings?

Yes, we offer discounts for large bookings. Please contact us for more details.

6. How do I receive my photos/videos?

Photos and videos are delivered digitally via email or through a cloud storage link.

7. Can I customize my package?

Absolutely! We can customize packages to suit your specific needs and preferences.

8. What happens if it rains on the day of my event?

We have backup plans for inclement weather, including indoor options or rescheduling.

9. How long will it take to receive my photos?

You can expect to receive your photos within 2-4 weeks after your event.

10. Can I see samples of your work?

Yes, you can view samples of our work in the gallery section of our website.
